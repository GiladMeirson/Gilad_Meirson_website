Summary of the project:
In order to learn about the platform, Firebase allows the transfer and backup of information to a secure server while using a real time database, I developed a website that transfers information to the server, in the project itself you can receive notifications of who is online and who is not, a fast and immediate chat! And even uploading posts openly or anonymously.

Website Instructions:
First you must register or log in. All you need is a name and password of your choice. After that there is a button with the plus sign, you can upload posts according to your request and view the posts of all users who have uploaded. On the button of the group you can see who is connected and who is not and by clicking on the user's square you can enter a private chat with him and all this in real time
