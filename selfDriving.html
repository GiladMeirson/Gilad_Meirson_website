Self-driving car with gentic algorithm and neural network

  • Gilad Meirson
  • #AI #neural network #gentic algorithm
  • 4.6

Summary of the project:
I really like doing genetic algorithms so I decided to follow a YouTube guide that teaches how to combine genetic algorithms and neural networks.
The project works as follows:
I generate hundreds of 300 cars at the beginning that don't know anything and the weights on the arcs of the grid are randomly drawn (the sensors).
The focus is always on the most advanced car,
when there is a car that has figured out how it should move to overtake the red cars,
its "brain" can be saved and then the next run will be its "children", that is, similar to it in terms of the weights on the network,
but slightly different neurons with a mutation of 0.15 on the weights.

Website Instructions:
The simulation starts automatically.
Above on the left is the distance of the best (most advanced) car.
If you liked the behavior of one of the cars, you can click on the SAVE icon to save its brain for next time.
Or if you want to delete then on the DELETE icon.
The blue arrow icon restarts the simulation with the brain you saved and if you haven't saved then randomly.
Enjoy!
