Summary of the project:
I think this was the first project I experimented with Canvas API, while doing the project I discovered amazing capabilities of the canvas which only gave me a taste for more. The game itself consists of 4 different difficulty levels! The project turned out well and rich in cool and physical animation

Game Instructions:
To play all you need at the beginning is to choose a difficulty level from 4 different difficulty levels. And to hit your enemies you have to aim at them with the mouse and hit them with the bullets you shoot. You don't have a limit on the amount of balls, but they only have one attempt not to die, as soon as one of the enemies touch you, you are disqualified! have fun
