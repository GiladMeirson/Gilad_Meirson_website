Summary of the project:
It's amazing how such a simple project yields such good results. I can spend hours creating different patterns through the generator I built. The construction of the generator was simple and easy but its results are crazy and can form the basis of an NFT product. Feel free to use it as you like, you can save the images and use them as you wish

Operating Instructions :
There are several inputs in the toolbar above:
Density: you can enter any number (integer, Irrational or negative) this parameter affects the shape of the pattern.
Radius: affects the size of the radius of each circle.
Line width: affects the thickness of the border of the circle.
Of course you can choose the colors for the border and filling of the circle.
Checkboxes:
Source on front: means that the first circle will always be in front, etc.
Colorful: Changes the fill color to the colors of the rainbow.
Get bigger: For each time the circle is drawn the radius increases.
