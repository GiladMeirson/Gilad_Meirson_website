Summary of the project:
MandelBrot Set is a set of complex numbers expressed in the Mandel Brot formula.
for more info
The essence of the project is to enable the illustration of the complex numbers within the Mandelbrot group through intelligent color manipulation, and calculation of the center point of the screen.
I'm personally addicted to the results and can't stop researching the Mandelbrot graph.

Operating Instructions :
*Clicking on a point on the canvas will make the center of the drawing and the canvas the point that was clicked, so you can "move" around the Mandelbrot.
By clicking on each solved plus, a toolbox will open for you,
where you can choose the calculation resolution (it is recommended to put it on the 800-900 area depending on your zoom).
Another thing is that you can choose to zoom in or zoom out and enter the mysterious world of the Mandelbrot. A small bonus on the ANIMATE button you can watch the animation of the Mandelbrot being formed
