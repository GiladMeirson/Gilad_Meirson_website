Old-School Snake Game

  • Gilad Meirson
  • #Web #Game #Canvas
  • 3.9

Summary of the project:
The goal of the project is to learn more about the Canvas API and try and improve the 2D physics technique. I really enjoyed making the game and even enjoyed playing it myself

Game Instructions:
Use the arrows to move the snake.
You can mute or play music at your pleasure.
If the snake touches itself you will be disqualified or if the snake touches the wall you will be disqualified.

Think you can get 550 points? If so, play now and try to break the record of the game!
